Canvas comes packed with features, yet continues to remain simple, flexible and easy to use.

  • edit

    Markdown

    Like writing with markdown shorthand syntax? You'll love the split-screen editor with built-in keyboard shortcuts.

  • invert_colors

    Theming

    Design and create a unique theme to give your blog a personal touch. We've already got the files in place.

  • date_range

    Scheduled Posts

    Draft posts and schedule them to publish exactly on the date and time you choose, or save your work for later.

  • comment

    Comments

    Disqus comments can easily be turned on or off. Engage your readers and followers with this powerful tool.

  • trending_up

    Analytics

    Native Google Analytics integration support. Just drop in your tracking id and start building reports today.

  • code

    Open Source

    Everything here is Open Source, so you can theme, extend and integrate to your heart’s content. Developers welcome.

Write faster and better than you ever have before with Canvas. Embrace the uncluttered simplicity that makes this platform stand above the rest.

Canvas is a simple alternative to platforms like WordPress, Medium or Tumblr. Focus on the stuff that really matters, like your content.

Canvas uses the power of Markdown with a live-preview, so you can format all of your posts on the fly with just a couple of extra keystrokes.

They say a picture says a thousand words, which is why it’s important for you to be able to quickly and easily upload images and media to Canvas.

Integrate with GitHub, Twitter, Facebook and more to take advantage of everything Canvas has to offer with its dynamic social profile features.

About

Canvas is a simple, powerful blog publishing platform that lets you to share your stories with the world. Its beautifully designed interface and completely customizable framework allows you to create and publish your own blog, giving you tools that make it easy and even fun to do.
